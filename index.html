📶 About the data sources

Flight details are entered by you. Weather and delay-risk info comes live from Open-Meteo (no account needed). Delay reason categories follow the US DOT / BTS classification (Air Carrier, Extreme Weather, National Aviation System, Late-Arriving Aircraft, Security).
